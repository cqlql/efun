Lottery

Every predict taken place in NFA extracts a portion of its total placed amount, which is converted into NFA currency and added to a lottery bank. The more predicts taken place, the more NFA in the bank. All the players who placed their predicts in that game will have opportunities to win that prize. The prize will be added up week on week until the winner found it.
